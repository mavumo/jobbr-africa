Top 5 Career Assessment Platforms for Job Seekers

Finding the right career path can be challenging. Whether you are new to the job market or looking to switch careers, these career assessment platforms can help you make informed decisions.

1. CareerExplorer

CareerExplorer offers a comprehensive assessment that evaluates your interests, personality traits, and skills to suggest suitable career paths.

2. 123 Career Test

This platform provides a free career test based on Holland Code (RIASEC) career interest assessment.

3. My Next Move

My Next Move is an interactive tool for job seekers and students to learn more about their career options.

4. O*NET Interest Profiler

The O*NET Interest Profiler helps you find out what your interests are and how they relate to the world of work.

5. CareerFitter

CareerFitter offers a career test that matches your personality to the best careers for you.

For a more tailored career assessment, we recommend the free career test provided by our partners over at Jobtest.org.
